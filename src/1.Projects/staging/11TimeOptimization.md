### Module 0: Review the whole list and either keep it, or revise it to make it fit where you want to go.  

The general stylistic gist of this time mgmt approach is ... *no excuses, no happy-talk bs* ... ***COMPETENCE in time mgmt discipline is your only currency and you have to OWN your time mgmt discipline to control and steadily improve the VALUE of that currency.*** *This in intended to be something like an autodidactic bootcamp approach for the self-reliant survivor who treats time as the ultimate scarce resource in a hostile universe. ## Modules 1–20 (Discipline and Ownership Diligence)

The best way to learn it is to **work through and master** the lessons **with intention** while implementing, eg as in exercising, don't just do a mindless push-up, but do whatever it takes to just get started and then focus each rep doing a more perfect push-up, as you self-coach and think about what you must do to make the next pushup even more perfect.

Do this audit or journaling exercise with with zero mercy to expose and amputate low-value activities that bleed your operational capacity, establishing the baseline self-reliance metric that every subsequent module in this course will measure against or optimize further. PAY CLOSER ATTENTION TO HOW YOUR DAY IS FUCKED AWAY!!! Be more uncompromising ... OWNING your time means ***extremist*** execution strategies to ***OWN*** everything about your precious moments of time mgmt discipline from Jocko Willink at [https://jockowillink.com/](https://jockowillink.com/) and Cal Newport’s Deep Work framework at [https://calnewport.com/](https://calnewport.com/). One vitally important part for me about Deep Work is accepting and optimizing the MONASTIC time mgmt philosophy, something that I call **emigrating to Unplugistan** to ***stop being tethered*** to a nagging *nothingizers*, such as any device or social media personna or an integrated development environment ... minimalist, ascetic lifestyle and workflow is about the kind of DEEP working that is only possible when you are landscaping or doing martial arts or completely unplugged -- that also means relaxing while a pickup or XUV hauls your fat carcass around. Avoid automotive culture and any kind of activity based on using the crutch of a transport devices. A **[monastic or even hermetic workflow](https://share.google/aimode/nK9ej606cQThCGBh9)** means ZERO physical meetings EVER and generally ONLY limited instances of asynch meetings otherwise, ie NO facetime or Zoom.
