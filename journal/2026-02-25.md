# **Manifesto for Christian Voluntaryism**

I do not go *to church* or to a place that passes a collection plate. The church is Christ's body, the community of believers imitating the Christ. Buildings or institutions are distractions from the faith.

I do not ask you to join any movement. Movements demand allegiance; almost always that allegiance is to some human figure who uses coercion and things like excommunication to render judgement. 

I ask that you honor your Creator by keeping your vows and Oaths as best you can, but do not hold grudges or allow evil to live rent free in your head -- forgive and move forward in grace.  

I am not waiting for the *kingdom of heaven*; I simply declare that kingdom is already alive in every true Christian's heart -- simply acknowledge the ideal and seek to to better understand what it means so that you may live in accordance with our Creator's will, ie don't wait for a Sunday School cartoon version heaven, start practicing it in your life now.

- Live voluntarily, imitate Christ
- Train to be able to defend when necessary
- Accept a minimalist peace-securing state
- Open source workflows, agentify life

I am a **Christian Voluntaryist**.  

I follow the One who never coerced a single soul. Jesus Christ extended the invitation of eternal life freely—“Come, follow Me”—without sword, tax, or threat. He taught in open fields, healed without billing, overturned tables only against temple profiteers who turned sacred space into forced commerce. His life was the ultimate model of **voluntary association**: love offered, never extracted. Imitating Him means rejecting every form of initiated force while embracing the courage to protect the innocent and the sacred. This is Christian Voluntaryism—the political outworking of “love your neighbor as yourself” and “do unto others as you would have them do unto you,” lived without the boot of the state on anyone’s neck.

I initiate no violence, no theft, no fraud. I seek no one’s approval, conversion, or applause. I am never lonely; my sufficiency is in Christ and the sovereign self He redeemed. I require no friends, only those who freely choose to walk beside me. My daily creed is simple and radical: **Leave others alone. Demand to be left alone.**  

This is the **Non-Aggression Principle**—not a modern invention, but the ethical spine of natural law and the Golden Rule made political. No man or group may initiate force against the person or property of another. Aggression is the original sin of politics; voluntary cooperation is the redeemed path.

Yet I am no pacifist. The same Christ who said “turn the other cheek” to personal insults also fashioned a whip and drove out aggressors. Self-defense is not only permitted—it is a moral duty. I remain capable of calibrated, decisive violence to protect life, liberty, and property, and to make any initiator—street predator or government official—regret the moment they crossed the line. Deterrence is righteous. Retaliation in defense restores justice; initiation of force destroys it. I will never start the fight, but I will finish it.

I reject the lust for a coercive state, yet I accept a **pragmatic minarchist** reality in this fallen world. A minimal constitutional framework—courts, professional law enforcement, and national defense—funded by the least coercive taxation necessary—secures the public good of ordered liberty so that the vast majority of human life can remain radically free. This is not ideal; it is a temporary concession, like the Israelites’ demand for a king when they already had the Law. For the foreseeable century and beyond, this bare shield prevents chaos while we build voluntary alternatives. Anything more is idolatry: the state as false god, taxation as ritual sacrifice of autonomy.

And now the exhilarating frontier: **the aggressive democratization of AI**.

It has never been a better time to be alive for high-agency image-bearers of God. The Creator gave us dominion and minds to subdue the earth. Today we extend that dominion by flinging open the source code of intelligence itself. Proprietary gatekeeping is the new temple moneychanging—closed models that concentrate power in the hands of a few while the many are kept dependent. I reject it utterly.

We must **democratize AI coding** with missionary zeal: open-source everything—models, tools, agentic frameworks, IDE-terminal unifications, hybrid local/cloud systems. Let @OPENcode, @OPENclaw, and every true builder flood the commons with premium, evolving instruments. Put control back in the hands of developers and their human agents. Embrace the **agentic Human-In-The-Loop (HITL)** revolution: AI that amplifies rather than replaces human will, creativity, and moral judgment. Every soul becomes more capable, more sovereign, more able to steward creation, create value, and glorify God through ingenuity.

This is techno-optimism baptized by Christian hope. Not blind faith in machines, but faith in the Creator who equips His people for every good work. By opening the source we imitate the open-handed generosity of Christ—freely you have received, freely give. We accelerate the maximum-likelihood path to an idealized future: billions of high-agency individuals voluntarily cooperating, defending what is theirs, innovating without permission, and leaving one another gloriously, peacefully alone.

The age of high agency has arrived. The tools are here. The choice is yours—freely.

No coercion. No exceptions. No turning back.

**We LIVE in Christian Voluntaryism as if in a beautiful field of opening flowers.** Consult [*Matthew 6:25-34,Luke 12:22-32*](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Matthew%206%3A25-34%2CLuke%2012%3A22-32&version=ASV) ... or, if you prefer other walks, think about how the Buddha talked about the lesson of the lotus OR maybe you like the Bhagavad Gita: *To those who worship Me, thinking of no other, who are constantly engaged in devotion, I provide what they lack and preserve what they have.*

If you wondering whose job it is to remediate effluent around you so that beautiful flowers might be cultivated, remember that most flowers thrive in nutrient-rich, decaying and improving soils, which might even appear to be polluted environments with heavy metals that the flower can bioaccumulate so that the soil can be less polluted ... if you can notice the ugliness, then CONGRATULATIONS! That means that you are also capable of separating out the part that does not belong in order to remediate the situation it into one of beauty. 

*Merely complaining and avoiding ugliness is for weak volunteers who can only gripe. Fixing stuff is your job. Thanks for volunteering. That's what admirable kinds of voluntaryism are all about!*

*Welcome to heaven on Earth!*
