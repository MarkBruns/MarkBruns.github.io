# Digital-First, AI-Driven Education Venture Philanthropy Checklist

This idea emphasizes auctions, game theory, marketplaces, financial/economic literacy (via direct participation in prediction markets, ludic economics, haggling, trading, refurbishing, growing crops, etc.), targeted at family entities with jr. high to college-age kids, parents, and grandparents.

### 1–50: Non-Tech / Business / Philanthropic / Educational Considerations

1. **CSOP Legal Structure**  
   **Description**: Customer-Supported Ownership Plan—equity ownership distributed to participating families based on patronage/usage (auctions, trades, learning activities) rather than employment; core to making customers literal owners.  
   **SWOT Integration**: Strength (deep loyalty + mission alignment via ownership); Weakness (securities/compliance complexity with minors); Opportunity (unique hybrid philanthropy-for-profit model); Threat (regulatory reclassification as public offering).  
   **Resource**: [CESJ Just Third Way Glossary – CSOP Definition & Overview](https://www.cesj.org/just-third-way-glossary/)

2. **ESOP/CSOP Hybrid Tax Policy**  
   **Description**: Tax treatment of customer-ownership in a non-profit-maximizing entity (pass-through taxation, deductions, potential zero corporate tax initially).  
   **SWOT Integration**: Strength (capital freed for impact/education); Weakness (IRS scrutiny on hybrids); Opportunity (S-corp-style tax shield adapted to CSOP); Threat (legislative changes to ownership-plan rules).  
   **Resource**: [S Corporation ESOPs – Tax Benefits & Compliance](https://www.esop.org/articles/s-corporation-esops.php)

3. **COPPA Compliance for Kid-Facing Platform**  
   **Description**: Verifiable parental consent, data minimization, and privacy controls for users under 13 in marketplace/auction/education tools.  
   **SWOT Integration**: Strength (family trust & safety signal); Weakness (consent friction in onboarding); Opportunity (differentiation as responsible edtech); Threat (fines & enforcement, esp. post-2026 updates).  
   **Resource**: [FTC Complying with COPPA – FAQs & Guide](https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/resources/complying-coppa-frequently-asked-questions)

4. **FERPA Alignment for School Partnerships**  
   **Description**: Rules for handling student education records when integrating platform data with schools or family programs.  
   **SWOT Integration**: Strength (enables deeper school-family scaling); Weakness (strict consent/sharing limits); Opportunity (institutional credibility); Threat (breach liability).  
   **Resource**: [U.S. Dept. of Education – FERPA for EdTech Vendors](https://studentprivacy.ed.gov/audience/education-technology-vendors)

5. **Venture Philanthropy Funding Model**

   **Resource**: [EVPA – A Practical Guide to Venture Philanthropy & Social Impact Investment](https://www.canopusfund.org/fileadmin/redaktion/Documents/A-Practical-Guide-to-VP-and-SI-29.01.2016.pdf)
