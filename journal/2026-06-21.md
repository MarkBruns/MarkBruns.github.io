# Radar Skill

This skill operationalizes the 25 Vital Signals into a repeatable, tool-driven daily intelligence process. It produces a concise, actionable radar report that surfaces changes in the physical and intellectual frontier without reliance on editorial narratives. Every signal prioritizes mechanisms where participants risk capital, reputation, or scarce attention.

## Purpose
Provide the disciple with ground-truth indicators of economic health, technological momentum, energy abundance, space industrialization, robotics/defense progress, and cultural sentiment. Reinforce resource stewardship by focusing attention on counted-cost data rather than manipulated content.

## When to Use
- At the start of the 4 a.m. Holy Hour or first focused block
- When the user requests "daily radar" or "run radar"
- Weekly deep-dive on Sunday for portfolio stewardship review
- Any time news consumption temptation arises

## Daily Procedure
1. Load this skill at session start.
2. Execute the 25-signal checklist in order using the specified tools and sources.
3. Note material changes (direction, magnitude, context) for each signal.
4. Synthesize into the Radar Report format below.
5. Save the report to the Second Brain (Obsidian or designated PKM location) with today's date.
6. Pray explicitly: "Lord, transform this intelligence into greater wisdom, gratitude, and faithful stewardship of the time and resources You have entrusted to me."
7. Flag any signal requiring deeper investigation for a later focused block.

## The 25 Signals – Tracking Methods & What to Record

**1. COMEX Copper Futures (HG)**  
Sources: COMEX settlement prices, LME warehouse stocks, CFTC COT report.  
Track: Daily settlement, 20-day change, commercial net length.  
Note: Rising price + expanding commercial longs = genuine capex signal.

**2. Fusion Preprints + Prediction Markets**  
Sources: arXiv physics.plasm-ph daily stats, Semantic Scholar velocity, Kalshi/Polymarket fusion contracts.  
Track: Submission count and citation momentum; probability on "Q>5 sustained 10+ minutes by 2028".  
Note: Surges or probability shifts >5 points.

**3. Kalshi/Polymarket Macro Contracts**  
Sources: Kalshi and Polymarket platforms (GDP, core CPI, unemployment).  
Track: Contract volumes, open interest, probability movement around data releases.  
Note: Direction and magnitude of shifts.

**4. Baltic Dry Index (BDI)**  
Sources: BDI daily value and sub-indices (Capesize, Panamax).  
Track: Level and 5-day change.  
Note: Sustained moves >10% in either direction.

**5. US Treasury 2s10s Yield Curve**  
Sources: Treasury.gov or FRED daily yields.  
Track: 2s10s spread and 10-year yield level.  
Note: Inversion status, steepening/flattening, and 5-day change.

**6. CBOE VIX and Term Structure**  
Sources: CBOE VIX spot and futures curve.  
Track: Spot level, VVIX, contango/backwardation status.  
Note: Spikes above 25 or sustained low readings <15.

**7. Bitcoin On-Chain Flows**  
Sources: Glassnode or CryptoQuant (exchange reserves, stablecoin issuance).  
Track: 7-day change in exchange balances and stablecoin mint/burn.  
Note: Material reserve drawdowns or issuance surges.

**8. TSMC Revenue & Semiconductor Book-to-Bill**  
Sources: TSMC monthly revenue release, SEMI book-to-bill.  
Track: MoM revenue change and book-to-bill ratio.  
Note: Sustained >5% MoM growth or contraction.

**9. Hyperscale Data-Center Electricity Load**  
Sources: ERCOT, PJM, and other ISO queue reports and demand attribution.  
Track: New large-load interconnections and data-center share of demand growth.  
Note: Quarterly queue additions or load attribution >2% of total.

**10. Shanghai Containerized Freight Index (SCFI)**  
Sources: SCFI weekly index.  
Track: Level and week-over-week change.  
Note: Sustained moves >15%.

**11. arXiv AI & Robotics Momentum**  
Sources: arXiv cs.AI, cs.RO, stat.ML daily submissions and Semantic Scholar velocity.  
Track: 7-day submission count and top-cited papers.  
Note: >20% week-over-week increase in any category.

**12. USPTO Patents (Robotics, Space, UAV, Fusion, Directed Energy)**  
Sources: USPTO weekly bulk data by class (B25J, space systems, etc.).  
Track: Grant counts in target classes.  
Note: 4-week rolling average change.

**13. VC Deployment in Frontier Sectors**  
Sources: PitchBook or NVCA quarterly reports segmented by space, robotics, fusion, defense.  
Track: Quarterly deployment totals and median valuations.  
Note: Material quarter-over-quarter shifts.

**14. Kalshi/Polymarket on Recession, Fed, Space & Defense Milestones**  
Sources: Specific contracts (recession probability, Starship milestones, UAV adoption, defense tech).  
Track: Probability and volume changes.  
Note: >5 point moves or new high-volume contracts.

**15. EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report**  
Sources: EIA Wednesday 10:30 a.m. release.  
Track: Crude and distillate inventory changes.  
Note: Builds/draws >2 million barrels or refinery utilization shifts.

**16. Orbital Launch Cadence & Satellite Population**  
Sources: Jonathan McDowell / SpaceTrack public logs.  
Track: Weekly launch attempts, success rate, net new active satellites.  
Note: Launch cadence >4/week or sudden drop in success rate.

**17. COMEX Gold Futures & Gold/Silver Ratio**  
Sources: COMEX gold settlement and ratio.  
Track: Price level and 5-day change in ratio.  
Note: Sustained moves >5% or ratio compression/expansion.

**18. USD/CNY Onshore vs Offshore Spread**  
Sources: Daily CNY fixings and CNH NDFs.  
Track: Onshore/offshore differential and forward points.  
Note: Persistent discounts or widening spreads.

**19. Corporate Credit Spreads**  
Sources: ICE BofA investment-grade and high-yield indices.  
Track: Daily spread levels and 5-day change.  
Note: Widening >20 bps in either index.

**20. Prediction Markets on Drone/UAV & Defense Milestones**  
Sources: Kalshi/Polymarket contracts on drone delivery, export controls, or defense tech.  
Track: Probability shifts and volumes.

**21. Regional Electricity Demand Attribution**

**23. Cultural Prediction Markets & Sports-Betting Odds**  
Sources: Kalshi, Polymarket, and regulated sports books on verifiable events.

**24. Hiring Demand in Aerospace, Robotics, Defense, Satellite Sectors**  
Sources: Indeed Hiring Lab and LinkedIn Economic Graph sector indices.  
Track: Posting volumes in target verticals.  
Note: >10% month-over-month change in any sector.

**25. Starlink / Satcom Metrics**  
Sources: SpaceX updates, regulatory filings, spectrum auction results, insurance market data.  
Track: Active terminal growth, ARPU trends, new spectrum awards.

## Pitfalls & Mitigations
- **Stale data**: Always verify the most recent release date before recording a change.
- **Over-interpretation**: Record only material moves; ignore noise below defined thresholds.
- **Single-source bias**: Cross-check at least two independent sources for every signal.
- **Confirmation bias**: Log both bullish and bearish movements with equal rigor.
- **Tool failure**: If a primary source is unavailable, note it and use the nearest equivalent (e.g., alternative freight index).
- **Scope creep**: Limit daily execution to the 25 signals; deeper dives are scheduled separately.
