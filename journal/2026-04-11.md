# 20 Point Personal Lifestyle Plan

The 20 points of my plan were generated by Grok, per my attempt to prompt, read the output, refactor the prompt, read again, and repeat ... as something of test of Grok [and my ability to prompt Grok]to see how well it could match/predict my real lifestyle. 

My ~$10/day of current expenses is slightly below what Grok estimates for this lifestyle; my plan implementation is certainly not anything like a totally remote, off-grid rural lifestyle -- it's a life lived in a simple, but spacious older home that looks something like a suburban home lived in by an old curmudgeon -- it looks pretty much exactly like what it is. 

It includes maximized garden and fully scuplted permaculture lot with edible landscaping. The home include wrestling mats, heavy bags, a weight room and areas for my indoor plants during the winter. The inside of the house is for disciplined multliple train-eat-nap/rest cycles of indoor time. 

In a nutshell, the lifestyle allows me to write and reflect, completely miminizes stress, maximizes physical resilience, and is designed for inducing a general toughening through outage readiness (weeks/months without utilities). 

Practically, this involves maintaining the house at minimum of 42F~47°F winter / and maximum of82F~87°F summer with minimal electric supplemental heating/cooling only during extremes for indoor work and dehumidification. It features a simple but nourishing diet centered on high-quality beef (especially ground beef) for protein and fat, supplemented by garden edible landscaping. 

All points remain rooted in biblical discipline, stewardship, and self-reliance while honoring your preferences.The point of this  lifestyle is to glorify God through disciplined body, mind, and land while preparing for hardship. Although it is a luxury, I do rely upon personal workstations and rentable compute for my personal education in AI engineering / agentic development work, but the computer, including Grok, as an essential tool for bible study as well as for news, knowledge, and a connection with what other humans around the world are up to.
