## 200-Module AI/ML Curriculum

Each module is one self-contained sentence designed for **~4 hours of focused work** (reading, coding, note-taking, and deeper exploration) ... it's probably best to develop a full script for the daily session each day, but it never hurts to "read ahead" and design the day's study based on the road ahead ... and, of course, that means revising the roadmap based on how the industry and personal interests change with significant new developments in the field. 

The modules are numbered sequentially so each one nests and builds directly on the knowledge from all previous modules, progressing logically from raw theory through practical implementation, system design, generative AI, and finally product/strategy and production deployment.  

You can treat this as a ready-to-use 200-day (or self-paced) roadmap; simply expand each sentence with the relevant book chapters, code repos, papers, or projects.
