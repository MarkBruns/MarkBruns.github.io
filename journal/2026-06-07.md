### Key Principles for This Monday
- **Portfolio first** — the 101PortfolioLifestyle.html is the command center. Make it brutally honest and actionable.
- **Journal as the record** — every decision and reflection goes in the Journal so the Second Brain actually works.
- **Prune aggressively** — if something is not serving the 45 focused hours or the Rule, mark it sunset or back-burner.
- **No new shiny objects** — stay inside these two sites today.
- **Communion at every meal** — explicit prayer that the Triune God turns the food into fuel for discipleship.

This plan gives you 7.5 hours of deep, prioritized work while protecting the Holy Hour and the body-temple rhythm.
